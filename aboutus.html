More Branches Worldwide

Establishment Of Blue Moon

The establishment of Blue Moon in 2000 marked not only the birth of a hotel but the inception of a vision to redefine the standards of luxury and create a global sanctuary for travelers seeking an extraordinary experience. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for hospitality, we embarked on a mission to craft an environment where every guest feels not just welcomed, but truly at home.

From that inaugural year in 2000, Blue Moon has continued to grow, expanding its footprint across the globe. The establishment of each new Blue Moon property is a celebration of the journey we've undertaken, the milestones we've achieved, and the countless memories we've created with our valued guests.
